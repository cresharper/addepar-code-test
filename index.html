Summer Social 2017

About the Event

Please RSVP By Tuesday, August 1st

The days are long and the weather is getting warmer, it's time for our annual Summer Social! This year we are hosting Summer Social at Flood Park in Menlo Park. Bring your family and friends to spend an afternoon enjoying great weather and delicious food. Summer Social is a family friendly event, guests of all ages are welcome to attend. We will have all the summer necessities: games, face painting, competitions, and tasty treats!

When Thursday, August 10th 4pm-8pm Where Flood Park 215 Bay Road Menlo Park, CA 94025

Location

We will be set up in the Oak and Manzanitia Picnic Areas located near the park enterance. In addition to the Summer Social activities, you'll have access to all 21 acres of the park, the softball fields, tennis courts, playground and horseshoes. Please note that this park does not allow animals. Only service dogs are allowed into the park.
